Declaration of Impartiality

The Board emphasizes the independence, impartiality, competence, confidentiality and professional ethics.
The Board is committed to:
-Safeguard professional ethics;
- Provide impartiality of operations through the implementation of an Executive Board/Impartiality Committee for Versys comprised of internal and external members, academic, commercial or consumers communities;
- Protect the confidentiality of all information obtained in the course of its activities;
- Safeguard the Organization against financial pressures that may influence the decisions;
- Ensure through training procedures and programs that all certification activities are held by competent personnel;
- Maintain the certification system in conformity with the requirements;
- Maintain independency of all parties involved in the certification process, assuring the non-discrimination and taking on full responsibility by the decisions related to issuance, maintenance, suspension and withdrawal of certification.
